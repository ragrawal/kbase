# Hamming Distance
* Hamming distance between two numbers is the number of positions at which the corresponding bits are difference. For instance hamming distance between 1 and 4 is 2 because:
	* 1 --> (0 , 0 , 0 , 1)
	* 4 --> (0, 1, 0, 0)
